Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Travel Partner mobile application (the “App”), published by Zia-e-akhtar Apps (“we”, “us”). It also applies to our other apps that state they are covered by this policy and that collect no personal data.

Summary

We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. The App has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics. It works fully offline after the one-time setup described below.

Information we collect

None. We do not ask for, collect, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, location, or device identifiers. Your favourite destinations and languages are stored only on your device and never leave it.

Microphone (Voice Translator)

The microphone is used only when you tap the mic in the Voice Translator, to convert your speech to text. Speech recognition is performed by your device's built-in speech service (Apple's Speech framework on iOS, the Google/OEM speech recognizer on Android). Depending on your device settings, that system service may process audio on-device or via its provider's servers — this is governed by Apple's or Google's privacy policy, not by us. We never record, store, or transmit your audio ourselves.

Translation

Text translation runs entirely on your device using Google ML Kit. The text you translate is not sent to any server by us. Language models (~30 MB each) are downloaded from Google once per language and then work fully offline.

Internet use

The App works fully offline for content you've already downloaded. It uses the internet only to:

  • download translation language models, one time per language, from Google;
  • load destination photos from Wikimedia Commons, cached on your device;
  • fetch currency exchange rates from a free public rate service (open.er-api.com), cached on your device with a timestamp so the converter keeps working offline from the last-known rates;
  • open Google Maps or your phone's dialer when you tap "Get Directions" or an emergency helpline number;
  • check, about once a month, for new or updated destination guides (see below).

Loading a photo, fetching rates, opening Maps/dialer, or the monthly content check shares your IP address with those services in the normal way any web request does. We do not receive, log, or store your IP address or any other data from these requests, and no personal information is sent.

Destination content updates

Approximately once a month, the App quietly checks a public file hosted on GitHub for new or updated destination guides — the same original content format bundled with the App, refreshed without requiring an app update. This is a standard, anonymous HTTPS request; the file is encrypted in transit and at rest to deter scraping, not to hide anything from you. We do not receive, log, or store your IP address or any other data from this request, and no personal information is sent. If the check fails for any reason (offline, no new content yet), the App simply continues with the destinations already on your device.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Third-party services

We do not integrate any analytics SDKs or advertising. The external services the App talks to — Google ML Kit (translation models), your device's speech/TTS engine, Wikimedia Commons (photos), open.er-api.com (currency rates), Google Maps, and GitHub (content updates) — are each described above and are subject to their own providers' privacy practices. If a future version of the App adds new third-party services (for example, advertising), this policy and the App's store listing will be updated before that version is released.

Travel & safety disclaimer

Destination guides, currency rates, "good to know" travel tips, and emergency helpline numbers shown in the App are for general informational purposes only and are not official, legal, medical, or financial advice. Always verify current visa, entry, safety, and exchange-rate information from official sources before you travel.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the App handles data (for example, if a future version introduces advertising), we will update this policy and the App's store listing before that version is released.
