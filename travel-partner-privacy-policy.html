Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Travel Partner mobile application (the “App”), published by Zia-e-akhtar Apps (“we”, “us”). It also applies to our other apps that state they are covered by this policy and that collect no personal data.

Summary

We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. The App has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics. It works fully offline after the one-time setup described below.

Information we collect

None. We do not ask for, collect, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, location, or device identifiers. Your favourite destinations and languages are stored only on your device and never leave it.

Microphone (Voice Translator)

The microphone is used only when you tap the mic in the Voice Translator, to convert your speech to text. Speech recognition is performed by your device's built-in speech service. Depending on your device settings, that system service may process audio on-device or send it to its provider's servers — this is governed by your device maker's privacy policy, not by us. We never record, store, or transmit your audio ourselves.

Translation

Text translation runs entirely on your device. The text you translate is not sent to any server by us. Language models are downloaded once per language and then work fully offline.

Internet use

The App works fully offline for content you've already downloaded. It uses the internet only to download translation language models, load destination photos, fetch currency exchange rates (cached on your device so the converter keeps working offline), open Maps or your dialer when you tap "Get Directions" or a helpline number, and check about once a month for updated destination guides. These requests share your IP address with the relevant service in the normal way any web request does. We do not receive, log, or store your IP address or any other data ourselves, and no personal information is sent.

Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Third-party services

We do not integrate any analytics SDKs or advertising. The external services described above are subject to their own providers' privacy practices. If a future version of the App adds new third-party services (for example, advertising), this policy and the App's store listing will be updated before that version is released.

Travel & safety disclaimer

Destination guides, currency rates, "good to know" travel tips, and emergency helpline numbers shown in the App are for general informational purposes only and are not official, legal, medical, or financial advice. Always verify current visa, entry, safety, and exchange-rate information from official sources before you travel.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the App handles data (for example, if a future version introduces advertising), we will update this policy and the App's store listing before that version is released.
